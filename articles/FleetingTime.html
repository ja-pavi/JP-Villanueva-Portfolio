Thoughts on fleeting time

Jacob Villanueva

JUN. 29, 2022

I often fear that I'm running out of time. That one day I'll wake up and realize my dreams have long passed me. I worry frequently about time, more so that I even take action against these fears. I worry so much on the things that I can't control, that I forget to make use of the things that I can control.

I think I am a glass half empty guy.

You could give me 24 hours, and I would stress for 24 of those hours that I don't have enough time, instead of using those hours to complete the given work. Maybe a time management problem or maybe just stuck in my own mind.

I think too much, just need to do.
